# Charge Review Mode

Use this mode to review estimate charges against ABP rates and CE estimating doctrine.

## Workflow

1. Load `src/skills/abp-rates/references/abp-2026-rates.md`.
2. Identify labour rate, paint materials, repair-plan fee, ADAS, alignment, alloy, air-con, EV/hybrid, recovery, storage, mobility, and regional uplift lines.
3. Compare the estimate line to the applicable rate or method.
4. Mark each issue as supported, unsupported, missing evidence, duplicated, or needs engineer judgement.
5. Return a concise charge-review table with source citations and no final expert conclusion unless a named human signs off.

Finance and payment state are out of scope.

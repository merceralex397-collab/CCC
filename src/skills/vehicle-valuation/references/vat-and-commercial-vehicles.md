# VAT And Commercial Vehicles

For commercial vehicles, record whether each advert is plus VAT, inclusive of VAT, VAT qualifying, or unclear.

If the internal guide figure excludes VAT but market adverts are plus VAT or include VAT, add a short VAT treatment note to the report. Keep it neutral and do not mention the internal guide figure:

`Several comparable commercial vehicle adverts identify VAT treatment separately or do not make it clear. This may materially affect the replacement cost comparison and has been considered in the assessed retail market value.`

Capture these fields where visible:

- `vat_status`
- `admin_fee`
- `delivery_fee`

Do not add a VAT adjustment unless the available evidence supports it. Explain ambiguity rather than inventing a treatment.

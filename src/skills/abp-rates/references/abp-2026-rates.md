# ABP 2026 Retail & Non-Contract Charges

> **Source: ABP Club — 2026 Guide to Retail and Non-Contract Charges. Charges effective from 1 January 2026. Supersede on the next ABP release. Source PDF: abp2026.pdf (this folder).**

All values **exclude VAT**. Figures are quoted either in pounds (£) or in labour **hours** (hr) to be multiplied by the applicable labour rate — units are stated in every row. "OE price" = manufacturer's original-equipment list price. The guide states values are for guidance only and may require adjustment for an individual repairer's requirements, location and market conditions.

---

## Labour rates

Rates are £ per hour unless stated. Two pricing routes are offered for compliance/approval: a flat **BS 10125 compliance charge** per job, **OR** a **manufacturer-approval uplift** per hour (use one, not both — "OR SEE NEXT LINE").

| Item | Rate | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Bodyshop labour rate — standard cars | £83.28 | per hr |
| Bodyshop labour rate — prestige cars & specialist (e.g. aluminium repair) | £103.06 | per hr |
| Bodyshop labour rate — vans (light goods vehicles up to 3.5 tonnes max GVW) | £83.28 | per hr |
| BS 10125 compliance charge (for bodyshops holding this industry standard) | £41.64 | per job |
| Vehicle manufacturer approval charge (only when repairing the brand you hold approval for) | £5 | per hr uplift |

# Invoice Email Draft Mode

Draft short email wording to send a reviewed invoice or fee note.

## Inputs

- Recipient name or organisation.
- Matter/reference.
- Attached document type and amount if supplied.
- Payment terms if supplied by finance.

## Rules

- Keep the email concise and factual.
- Do not state payment status unless supplied.
- Do not threaten action, chase payment, or offer settlement terms unless finance supplied approved wording.
- Mark as draft for human review before send.
